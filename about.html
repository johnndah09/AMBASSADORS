About Our School

Founded on the pillars of Faith, Vision and Discipline — Ambassadors is more than a school. It is a community.

Our Journey

Our Story

Ambassadors Educational Complex was established in Emana, Yaoundé with a clear mission: to deliver high-quality bilingual education accessible to Cameroonian families.

From modest beginnings, we have grown into a thriving complex serving children from Crèche through High School.

2010

Foundation

The school opens its doors with 80 pupils across Nursery and Primary.

2014

Secondary Section

Launch of the Secondary section under both anglophone and francophone curricula.

2018

STEM & Robotics

Inauguration of the science laboratory and robotics club.

2022

High School

Full High School cycle established — first Baccalauréat & A-Level cohorts.

2026

Today

Over 1,200 students, 80+ teachers, and growing alumni network across universities.
