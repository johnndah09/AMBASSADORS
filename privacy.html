Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Ambassadors Educational Complex ("AEC", "we", "our") respects the privacy of our learners, parents, staff and website visitors. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it and the rights you have over your information, in accordance with applicable Cameroonian data protection law and good international practice.

1. Information we collect

  • Identification: name, date of birth, address, photographs of learners (with parental consent).
  • Contact: phone numbers, email addresses, emergency contacts.
  • Academic: grades, attendance, behaviour records, health declarations.
  • Financial: payment history, transaction references (we do not store card or Mobile Money PINs).
  • Web data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via Google Analytics).

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver education and pastoral care.
  • To communicate with parents about school life, fees and emergencies.
  • To process admissions and tuition payments.
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations under Cameroonian education law.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Sharing your information

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with: official Ministry of Education bodies, certified examination boards, our payment partners (MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, banks) and our authorised technology providers — under strict confidentiality.

4. Data security

We use HTTPS encryption, role-based access, regular backups and audit logging to protect personal data. Access to learner records is restricted to authorised staff and parents/guardians.

5. Your rights

You may at any time request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements. Contact privacy@ambassadors-edu.cm.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to remember your language preference and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@ambassadors-edu.cm or call +237 677 637 429.
