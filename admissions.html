Admissions

We welcome families who share our values and want the best for their children. Apply in three simple steps.

How to Apply

Three steps to join the family

1

Step 1 — Submit Application

Complete the online application form below or download the PDF. Provide academic records and birth certificate.

2

Step 2 — Entrance Assessment

Children from Class 2 onwards take a short placement test. Younger children attend a friendly observation session.

3

Step 3 — Family Interview & Confirmation

Parents meet with the Head of School. Once admitted, pay the registration fee to confirm placement.

Required Documents

Required Documents

Completed application form
Birth certificate (copy)
Most recent school report card
Two recent passport photographs
Vaccination record
Transfer certificate (if applicable)

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year begin?
The school year starts in early September and ends in late June, structured into three terms.
Do you offer scholarships?
Yes — merit and need-based scholarships are awarded each year. Contact admissions for the application form.
What language is used in class?
Our Anglophone and Francophone sections each follow the official Cameroonian curriculum, with cross-language enrichment classes.
Is there transport?
Yes, secure school transport is available across Yaoundé. Routes and fees are listed on the Boarding & Transport page.
How are children placed in the right class?
Through a short, age-appropriate placement assessment in core subjects, plus a meeting with parents. We aim for the level where the child will thrive — neither too easy nor too challenging.
Are there sibling discounts?
Yes — families enrolling 2 or more children receive an automatic discount on tuition. Contact admissions for the current rates.
