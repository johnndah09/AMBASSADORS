Academic Programs

From the first lullaby in the Crèche to the final Baccalauréat exam — every step of the journey is designed for excellence.

01

Crèche & Nursery

1 – 5 yearsBilingual immersionPlay-based

What we focus on

Our youngest learners are nurtured in a warm, secure environment where curiosity is encouraged and language skills bloom naturally. Days are structured around play, stories, songs and gentle guided activities in both English and French.

  • Daily routines that build confidence and independence
  • Phonics, early literacy and number sense
  • Music, art, dance and dramatic play
  • Christian values stories and morning prayer
  • Outdoor play and motor skills development

A typical day

  • 7:30 – Welcome & free play
  • 8:00 – Morning circle and assembly
  • 9:00 – Guided learning (alphabet / numbers)
  • 10:00 – Snack & outdoor break
  • 10:30 – Bilingual story time
  • 11:30 – Creative arts
  • 12:30 – Lunch & quiet rest
  • 14:00 – Songs, games and pickup
02

Primary School

6 – 11 yearsClass 1 to Class 6 / CP to CM2Cameroon Anglophone & Francophone

Curriculum focus

Our Primary years build deep mastery of reading, writing, mathematics and reasoning, while opening minds to science, history, geography, civic education and the arts.

  • English Language Arts & French Language
  • Mathematics with hands-on problem solving
  • Environmental & Social Studies
  • Religious & Moral Education
  • Computer literacy & coding basics
  • Physical Education, music, art

Assessment & support

Continuous assessments combined with end-of-term examinations track every child's progress. Personalized feedback to parents three times per year.

  • Weekly quizzes and class projects
  • Termly written examinations
  • Reading recovery and maths catch-up sessions
  • Reading club, spelling bees, math olympiads
  • End-of-cycle CEP / FSLC preparation
03

Secondary School

11 – 16 yearsForm 1 – Form 5 / 6e – 3eBEPC, Probatoire, GCE O-Level

Subjects offered

  • English & French (advanced)
  • Mathematics & Further Mathematics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • History, Geography, Citizenship
  • Computer Science & Digital Skills
  • Economics, Commerce, Accounting
  • Religious Studies / Ethics
  • Sports & Physical Education

Pathways

Students are guided into the best path — Sciences, Arts, or Commerce — based on their strengths, interests and future aspirations. Our counselors meet each family before stream selection.

  • BEPC preparation (Francophone)
  • GCE O-Level preparation (Anglophone)
  • Probatoire / GCE A-Level entrance
  • Career exploration workshops
  • University fairs and mentoring
04

High School

16 – 18 yearsLower & Upper Sixth / 1ère & TleGCE A-Level, Baccalauréat

Examination preparation

Our high school cycle prepares students for the most rigorous national and international examinations. Past-paper drills, mock exams and university interview coaching are part of every term.

  • Baccalauréat A, C, D, G series
  • GCE A-Level Sciences, Arts and Commerce
  • SAT and TOEFL preparation (optional)
  • University application support
  • Scholarship counseling

Beyond the curriculum

  • Model United Nations & debate club
  • Robotics, coding and entrepreneurship lab
  • Community service & leadership program
  • Internship partnerships with local industries
  • Alumni mentorship network
  • Annual academic & cultural trips
